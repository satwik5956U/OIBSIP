Biography

Steve paul "steve" jobs was an american information
technology entrepreneur and inventer. He was the
cofounder, chairman, and the ceo of apple inc, CEO and
largest shareholder of pixar Animation studios; and
the founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT Inc.Jobs is widely
recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution
of the 1970s, along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.
"Creative entrepreneur" whose passion for perfection and
ferocious drive revoltionized six industries:personal
computers, animated movies, phones, tablet
computing, and digital publishing
